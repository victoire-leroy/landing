"Le Baiser"

Le Baiser is a painting by Austrian painter Gustav Klimt, created between 1908 and 1909. This oil-on-canvas painting covered in gold leaf is housed in the Belvedere Palace in Vienna. The work is part of Klimt's Golden Cycle, and is surely the Austrian painter's most famous work.

La Jeune Fille à la perle is an oil-on-canvas painting by the Dutch painter Johannes Vermeer circa 1665. This medium-sized painting is a bust portrait of an anonymous young woman - perhaps one of Vermeer's daughters - wearing a pearl in her ear and a turban on her head.
